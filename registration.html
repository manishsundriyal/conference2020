Registration

Interested participants are requested to fill up the registration form and send along with the registration fee to the Organizing Secretary. The registration fee per participant is to be paid by NEFT/Net banking/IMPS/Demand Draft, drawn in favor of “EICEEEC-2020” and payable at Delhi. The details of the registration fee are as given below:

Category Fee
Industry Participants Rs. 8000
Participants from R&D/Academic institution Rs. 6000
Student Participants Rs. 5000
Foreign Participants US$ 250
