Paper Submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit papers of 6-8 pages(A4) including tables, figues and references, although if you have a paper with more number of pages that you would like to be considered you can submit that as well, All submissions must be made in the given SSRN-Elsevier format. A blind peer review process will be used to evaluate all the submitted papers. Each full registeration will cover a maximum of one paper.Extra paper, Second Paper onweards, must be registered separately.
The formal instructions in the template must be followed, It is important to use the right paper format is A4 to have right margin, not to use page numbering (paper footer must be empty). All submissions should be written in english with a maximum paper length of 8 pages inclusding figures. Additional pages will be charged 20 USD per page.

Guide Lines for Authors

  • Authors must submit their papers online using easy chair conference management system. Authors should first create an account on Easy Chair to log on. The Submission website for IEEEC2021 is IEEEC 2021 Manuscript should be submitted in Doc. and pdf format both
  • All the papers tat confor,, to the guidelines will be peer reviewd and evaluated based on originality content/depth, relevance to conference, contributions and readability. Acceptance of the pepers will be communicated by email. The authors of the accepted paper shall have to make corrections as per the suggestions of the reviews adn submit the final camera ready manuscript within the stipulated deadline.
  • Do not use academic titles such as Dr., Prof. etc in teh paper
