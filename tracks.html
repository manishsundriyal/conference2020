Image, Speech and Signal Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, Robotics, and Control Sensors, Embedded Systems Design, Hardware Impletation, Control Systems.

Instructions for Authors

  • Prospective Authors are invited to submit original technical papers for publication in IEEEC 2021
  • Papers will be selected on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conferece at the same time. These matter will be reported to SSRN-Elsevier and strict actions will be taken against whoever engages in these activities
  • An Author of an accepted paper is requiered to register for the conference and present the paper at the conference. All accepted will be submitted for consideration to SSRN-Elsvier
  • Registeration fee must be paid before the due date of the registeration, For authors with multiple papers one registeration for each paper is required
  • Registeration Fee is Non-Refundable
